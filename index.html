SAFEIV

Safeiv is a desktop application that extracts behavioral analysis of the applicants in online interviews. It uses face expression, body language, and eye movements. Applicants are analysed in terms of whether they cheat by using the external sources along with their mood, level of excitement, and self-confidence.
